About


I like to learn new technologies and make my work have a positive impact on people's lives.


From my first steps in the 1980s with a Commodore 64 computer to my current developments in web systems, I have embarked on an exciting journey that has allowed me to grow personally and professionally.

My beginnings in computing go back to the time of the legendary Commodore 64 . It was my first contact with this wonderful machine and the BASIC V2 programming language. I remember spending hours writing lines of code, experimenting with the capabilities of the C64, and developing small programs.

As I progressed on my path as a programmer, I also delved into the world of assembly language, creating low-level code for the Commodore 64 and its successor, the C128. It was a challenging but rewarding stage that allowed me to gain a deeper understanding of the inner workings of computers.

As the 1990s progressed, I began exploring other programming languages and platforms. During this time, I worked with IBM 386, 486, and 586 computers, as well as Tandy PC computers in their monochrome and color versions. My experience with these machines helped me expand my knowledge and adapt to different development environments.

Throughout all these years, I have had the opportunity to constantly develop applications and programs for both clients and friends. My experience ranges from desktop application development to web page creation. I have worked with various programming languages and technologies to meet the needs of my clients and friends.

Throughout my journey, I have had the opportunity to use a wide range of operating systems. From the early days of GEOS and Windows 1.0 to the latest version, Windows 11. Currently, I base my work on the Xubuntu Linux system, which provides me with a stable and versatile platform for developing applications and web systems.

With the advent of the global pandemic, I have focused a large part of my efforts on web system development. I have immersed myself in technologies such as HTML5, JavaScript, CSS, React, Node.js, PHP, and PostgreSQL. These tools have allowed me to create modern and efficient web solutions for my clients. To me, each client is more than that, they are a friend, and my main focus is to help them grow and achieve their goals.

In summary, my journey in the world of computing and programming has been fascinating and full of constant learning. From my first steps with the Commodore 64 to my current developments in web systems, I have acquired a diverse set of skills and knowledge. My main goal is to use my experience and passion for programming to help individuals and businesses.
